- `styles.css` — styles

How to customize
1. Replace "BetaRadz" with your full name or brand.
2. Update the hero text, bio, and social links in `index.html`.
3. Replace sample projects: change image, title, data-desc, data-tech, and add `data-link` for live/demo or repo.
4. Swap colors and fonts in `styles.css` (root variables at top).
5. Replace `you@example.com` with your email in the contact form and mailto links.
